# /marketing — Yummy Garden Marketing

Generate marketing content for the Yummy Garden app.

## Context

Yummy Garden is a free, cute meal-tracking app that helps users (primarily women) build healthy eating habits by growing a virtual garden. Every meal logged plants a flower. Aesthetic: fruits (🍓🫐🍇🥭🍌), rainbows, sunshine, butterflies, flowers. Soft pastels. Never about calorie counting, restriction, or guilt.

**URL**: Ask the user for the live URL if not known.
**Repo**: https://github.com/timmyers/yummy

## What to generate

Pick ONE based on what would be most useful right now, or what the user asks for:

### 1. Social Media Posts
Generate 3-5 ready-to-post captions for different platforms:
- **Instagram/TikTok**: Short, emoji-rich, with hashtags. Hook + value + CTA.
- **Twitter/X**: Punchy, under 280 chars, conversational tone.
- **Reddit**: Authentic, community-oriented (r/selfcare, r/ADHD, r/webdev for the tech angle)

Tone: warm, playful, relatable. Speak to the pain ("forgetting to eat", "too busy to take care of myself") and the joy ("watching my garden bloom").

### 2. Landing Page Copy
Generate headline, subheadline, 3 feature bullets, and CTA button text. Hero section only.

### 3. App Store / Product Hunt Description
Short description (80 chars), long description (500 chars), and 3 key selling points.

### 4. Growth Ideas
Brainstorm 5 specific, actionable growth tactics for this app and audience. Consider:
- Communities where the target audience hangs out
- Partnerships (wellness influencers, ADHD creators, self-care accounts)
- Content marketing angles
- Viral mechanics already in the app (share card, achievements)

### 5. Email / Newsletter
Draft a short launch announcement email (3 paragraphs max).

## Principles

- **Never mention calories, weight, dieting, or restriction.** This is about nourishment and self-care.
- **Relatable over aspirational.** "I forgot to eat again" > "optimize your nutrition."
- **Inclusive.** All bodies, all foods, all eating patterns are valid.
- **Playful.** Emoji-friendly, warm, a little whimsical.
- **Honest.** It's a simple free app, not a life-changing product. Undersell, overdeliver.
